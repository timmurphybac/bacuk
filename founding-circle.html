Founding Circle

A Small Circle Around a Big Purpose

Every community contains people who care.

Some bring wisdom. Some bring encouragement. Some bring experience. Some simply bring belief.

The Founding Circle exists to stand beside the Chairperson of Heart and the first family.

It is not a committee. It is not a board. It is not a governing body.

It is a circle of people who wish to see dignity grow.

Members of the Founding Circle encourage, support, celebrate and help sustain the journey through each milestone.

The first milestone comes first. Then the next. Then the next.

Results first. Recognition later.

One family.
One relationship.
One act of dignity at a time.
