Chairperson of Heart

The First Act of Dignity

Somewhere in every community there is a person who sees.

They have stature.

They have courage.

They do not stand back.

They do not seek recognition.

They simply understand that when a family is ready to walk into dignity, somebody must be willing to walk beside them.

This person is the Chairperson of Heart.

The community recognises them.

Volunteers trust them.

Community partners recognise their character.

Their authority does not come from position.

Their authority comes from compassion, integrity and action.

They are the baton which passes the gift of dignity.

The Chairperson of Heart helps identify Family One.

They walk with that family.

They ensure that dignity is not discussed as an idea but experienced as a reality.

This single act becomes the cornerstone of distinction.

Not a programme.

Not a campaign.

A relationship.

One family. One act. One milestone.

Only when this act is complete do we consider the next milestone.

Community ONE advances through demonstrated dignity, never through aspiration alone.

Find the first family. Walk with them. Then begin again.
