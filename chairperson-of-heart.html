Chairperson of Heart

The Person Who Sees

Somewhere in every community there is a person who sees.

A person of stature. A person of compassion. A person who does not stand back when dignity can be given.

They do not seek recognition. They do not seek attention. They do not seek acclaim.

They simply wish to help a family walk into dignity.

When this person comes forward, a community recognises something important: trust, character and stewardship.

The first act is simple.

Find the first family.
Walk with them.
Light dignity.

Only then do we consider the next milestone.

This becomes a repeatable act of depth, a cornerstone of distinction, and a bridge built one family at a time.

The Chairperson of Heart is not a title of authority. It is a title of service.

The baton is passed. The gift is given. The family is welcomed. The bridge begins.
