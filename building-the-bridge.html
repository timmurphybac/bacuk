Building the Bridge

The moment a community becomes willing.

Community ONE begins when local willingness, volunteer energy, community trust and human dignity meet in one place.

Communities are full of people who care.

Some have compassion. Some have experience. Some have energy. Some have influence. Some have practical skills.

Most simply need an invitation.

When a community becomes willing to welcome volunteers, local initiative and a Collection Point, something remarkable becomes possible.

A bridge begins to form.

The volunteer brings effort.

The Chairperson of Heart brings compassion, stature and encouragement.

The Collection Point creates a place of participation.

The community-based provider creates a place of belonging.

The first family receives dignity.

Each participant contributes something different. Together they create something none could create alone.

This is the bridge.

BAC exists to encourage, preserve and replicate these moments. Not by commanding them. Not by owning them. But by helping communities recognise what is already present within themselves.

We believe the greatest resource in any community is not money.

It is a willing human being.

One volunteer. One act. One relationship. One family. Then another. And another.

Until a community discovers what it is capable of becoming.
